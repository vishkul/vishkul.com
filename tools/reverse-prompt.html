// reverse-prompt · experiment

Paste AI text.
See its prompt.

Everyone reverse-engineers AI text the wrong way. Feed it the output, and this works backward to the input: the task, the tone, the format, the persona, and the shape someone probably asked for. You get a reusable prompt out the other side.

try a sample runs locally · nothing stored
~/tools/reverse-prompt

Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored. This infers a likely prompt from the writing's fingerprints; it cannot recover the exact words someone typed, because that information is not in the text.
